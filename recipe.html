---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Description

Three minute cakes that are loaded with protein and healthy ingredients.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredients

  • rolled oats
  • egg whites
  • protein powder
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Directions

  1. Start by adding the ingredients into a blender
  2. Let the batter rest for 5 minutes to allow it to thicken
  3. Finally scoop and pour the batter into a heated pan
  4. Cook over medium heat for about 3 to4 minutes on each side
